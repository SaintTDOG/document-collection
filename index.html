Your Client Needs Analysis

The form you're about to complete is called an NCCP Client Needs Analysis. It's a legally required record of your personal, financial and employment situation that Homeowner Assist and our lending partner rely on to decide whether the loan you're applying for is genuinely suitable for you. The National Consumer Credit Protection Act (the "NCCP" in the name) requires it, and the lender cannot assess your Assessment in Principle without it.

It exists to protect you. By answering every question accurately, you give the lender a complete picture of your circumstances — your income, your commitments, your goals — so they can make sure the loan structure won't put you under avoidable financial stress. It's also the document we refer back to if anything about your situation is ever questioned later, so it needs to reflect the truth of where you're at right now.

Please fill every field carefully. A single blank, a debt that slipped your mind (including buy-now-pay-later accounts like AfterPay, Zip or Humm, store cards, or HECS/HELP), or a name that doesn't exactly match your ID is enough for the lender to push the whole application back to us for correction — and every correction round typically adds one to two weeks to your purchase timeline. Completeness genuinely protects your move-in date as much as it protects the lender.

Set aside about 20–25 minutes and complete it in one sitting — the form can't be saved and returned to. Before you start, have your bank details, a list of your debts (including credit cards, BNPL and student loans), and your employer's contact details handy. If something doesn't apply to you, choose N/A, Unknown or Unsure from the dropdown rather than leaving the field empty.
